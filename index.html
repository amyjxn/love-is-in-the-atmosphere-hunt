Chicago IRL Clue Hunt

How it works:

  • Go to each location listed below in Chicago.
  • When you’re nearby, tap “Check My Location”.
  • If you’re close enough, the clue will unlock on this page.

Tip: Make sure location services are ON and you’re using your phone’s browser.

Clue #1

Navy Pier Ferris Wheel

Head toward the Ferris wheel that touches the sky. Once you're in the area at Navy Pier, tap the button below to unlock your clue.

Unlocked clue:
Look for the wheel that touches the sky—the one you can see from half the city. Your clue is nearby, if you give the area a quick spin...
Clue #2

Lincoln Park Zoo – Pritzker Entrance

Make your way to the Lincoln Park Zoo entrance that shares a name with the governor. When you’re outside the gate, tap the button to unlock your clue.

Unlocked clue:
Growl, roar, chirp, hiss, croak! Stand outside the entrance that shares a name with our governor. Your free ticket is almost in your paws!
Clue #3

Cloud Gate (The Bean)

Go to Millennium Park and find the reflective sculpture everyone photographs. When you can see yourself in it, tap below to unlock your clue.

Unlocked clue:
There's never BEAN a more iconic piece of art! When you spot your reflection, your next step is close at hand.
Clue #4

Chicago Theatre

Find the marquee that lights up State Street with the city’s name in bright lights. Once you’re there, tap to unlock your clue.

Unlocked clue:
All the world's a stage! When you see “CHICAGO” shining above you, your clue is just steps away.
Clue #5

Buckingham Fountain

Head to Grant Park and find the fountain that looks like it could spray the stars. When you’re nearby, tap below to unlock your final clue.

Unlocked clue:
Is that a whale? No, but it spouts like one! Near this monument in Grant Park, your search comes full circle.
Having trouble? Make sure location services are enabled and try moving closer to each landmark.
